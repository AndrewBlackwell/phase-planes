(Better) Phase Plane Plotter

Usage

  • a is a parameter variable for bifurcation analysis
  • Implicit multiplication: 2x = 2*x
  • Functions: sin, cos, tan, exp, log, sqrt, abs
  • Powers: x^2 or x**2
  • Click canvas to trace individual paths
  • On slide: auto-redraw when dragging a
